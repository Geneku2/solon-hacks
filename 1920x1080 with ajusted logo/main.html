What is Solon Hacks?
Solon Hacks is a non-profit Hackathon, a competition centered around utilizing code to solve real-world problems. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming and productive learning environment where students of all ages can build and share their creations and further develop a passion for computer science.
What is a "Hackathon"?
A Hackathon is a coding competition in which students
will be given a theme and a set time period to create an
app, website or game. The sky's limit as to what you
can create, as long as your within bounds of the theme.
What if I can't code?
That's okay! This program welcomes people with various skill
levels in computer science. Even if you come in with no knowledge,
the workshops within the hackathon will teach some essential programming skills and your teammates will also be of great help.
How big can our teams be?
Teams can be as big as 5 people, however if you
rather participate alone that is perfectly OK. Also if you
are looking to find teammates, our discord page
will give you an opportunity to search for other open teams.
Who is allowed to join?
This Hackathon is open to all 8th -12th graders,
regardless of what school you go to!
How do I sign up?
Go to the "Sign Up!" section on this website. There you

will have to fill out a google form and join our discord

page for important announcements regarding the
competition.
What's in it me?
Top placers in the Hackathon will be recieving
cash prizes and will be put on the front cover of
this webite! This is also an excellent opportunity
to learn and show what you know.

When is it?
The opening ceremony is on April 3rd and
competition ends on April 10th. Exact start
and end times will be announced on our
discord page as we get closer to the date,
so make sure to join us!
